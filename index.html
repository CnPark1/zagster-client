About

These Graphs show the amount of zagster rides taken per month. Looking at it shows that the top usage of the bikes come during the summer and the beginning of the school year. It then tapers off when winter starts, understandably. The data was pulled and printed from the zagster service (herokuapp.com) and the graphs are from chart.js.
